What is Product Management System?

Product Management System is a computer-based system for tracking Product levels, orders, sales and deliveries. It can also be used in the manufacturing industry to create a work order, bill of materials and other production-related documents.

Looking for product management software? Accompany offers one of the top product management tools for PM teams to manage requirements & feature requests

• Save time involved in gathering and maintaining requirements that come from many sources via different mediums such as emails, phone calls, meetings, etc.
• Capture, track and share requirements easily over the web – access securely from any computer connected to the internet.
• Avoid project delays/failures that can be caused when managing requirements using general purpose tools such as Word, Excel, and wikis.
• Collaborate with customers and internal stakeholders – to understand & meet customer needs consistently and build more successful products.
